Call for Posters

The SoCC poster sessions provide an opportunity to present works-in-progress in an informal setting. Topics of interest are the same as the research topics in the SoCC conference call for papers. While anyone can submit a proposal, preference will be given to posters where the primary contribution is from one or more students, or from an industry/start-up organization. Students must present student posters at the conference.

Poster Submission

Authors and students are invited to submit posters to communicate the essence of a research problem - why it matters, usage of techniques, any initial results, and the novelty and importance.

Submission Guidelines

Accepted posters will be A0 paper size (841 x 1189mm); portrait mode is recommended. We strongly encourage following this guidance on Creating an effective poster, and if possible include a URL providing additional information about your work to the attendees. When submitting, indicate if the submission should be considered for the student or industry/start-up best-poster award.

Review Process

Submissions are single blind, so please include authors' names and affiliation. By submitting a poster, you grant SoCC permission to post the poster on this web site, if accepted. (You'll be given a chance to modify it first.)
